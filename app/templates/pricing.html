FAQ

Common buying questions

What happens after I choose a plan? ⌄
You’ll complete a short guided setup, review your configuration, and pay securely online. Your initial workflow is then created automatically so you can get started right away.
Do I need technical experience to use this? ⌄
No. Roofing Front Desk is designed for roofing owners and office teams. The setup is guided, simple, and built to get you live without technical work.
Is phone and texting included? ⌄
Yes. Normal phone and messaging usage is included in Pilot and Growth, so you don’t need to manage a separate phone or messaging bill just to get started.
What’s the difference between Pilot and Growth? ⌄
Pilot is intentionally limited for proving value in one service area with one simple workflow. Growth unlocks the full operating system — deeper qualification, routing, team handoff, pipeline, and reporting.
Can I upgrade later? ⌄
Yes. Many companies start with Pilot to validate the workflow, then move to Growth once they want the full system running across more of their operation.

    FAQ

    Common buying questions

    What happens after I choose a plan?
    After you choose a plan, you’ll complete a short setup flow with your business details, service area, and workflow preferences. Then you’ll review your order and pay securely online. After payment, we configure your system and send your rollout steps.
    Do I have to set everything up myself?
    No. You provide your business and workflow details during checkout, and we handle the actual configuration. Roofing Front Desk is not instant self-install software — we set it up for your business after purchase so it’s ready to run properly.
    Are Twilio phone and messaging costs included?
    Twilio usage is billed separately based on your phone number, call forwarding, and text volume. Your Roofing Front Desk subscription covers the software, workflow logic, inbox, dashboard, and rollout support.
    Can I upgrade or customize later?
    Yes. Many companies start with Pilot to validate the workflow, then upgrade to Growth once they want more service areas, deeper branching, or higher lead volume. If your workflow is more advanced, we can scope a Custom rollout.
    How long does rollout take after payment?
    Most Pilot and Growth rollouts are configured and ready within a few business days after purchase, depending on workflow complexity and how quickly we receive any final business details.
