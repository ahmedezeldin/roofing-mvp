FAQ

Questions roofing owners will ask

Do Twilio costs count separately?
Yes. Messaging and phone usage can be passed through separately depending on how the account is configured.
Can the workflow be customized?
Yes. Pilot gives you a basic qualification flow. Growth adds deeper branching, routing, and more operational flexibility.
Do I need to set it up myself?
No. Both plans include setup help, but Growth is designed for a more guided live rollout.
Can I see the product before committing?
Yes. Explore the demo first, then start setup when you’re ready.
