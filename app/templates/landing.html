The problem

Most roofing companies do not have a lead problem. They have a follow-up problem.

01
Missed calls happen constantly
Storm days, weekends, evenings, and busy dispatch windows create missed opportunities that never get recovered properly.
02
Leads go cold fast
If nobody follows up quickly, the customer usually calls the next roofer on their list and your team loses the shot.
03
Office follow-up is inconsistent
Sticky notes, inbox overload, and manual callbacks make it hard to know what was answered, qualified, booked, or lost.
What the product does

Roofing Front Desk turns missed-call recovery into a real operating system.

Recover after-hours missed calls
Customers hear from your company right away instead of waiting for a callback that may never happen.
Pre-qualify leads before callback
Collect job type, urgency, postal code, and insurance status before the lead reaches your rep.
Give staff one clean inbox
Your team sees conversations, recommended next actions, notes, and status updates in one place.
Track leads from missed call to booked estimate
Move leads from new to qualified to booked to closed so nothing disappears into a black hole.
Reduce manual callback chaos
Adjust automated follow-up steps, handoff logic, and operator workflows around how your company actually runs.
